About us

Thanks for visiting to Our Hiji City Page

As students of Ritsumeikan Asia Pacific University (APU), we created this website to share the many reasons why Hiji City is an incredible place to live and visit. Whether you are a fellow APU student looking to explore the local area or a visitor seeking a unique Japanese experience, our aim is to provide you with insights and inspiration about this charming town. Hiji offers a harmonious blend of traditional culture, natural beauty, and a welcoming community that we believe everyone should experience.
