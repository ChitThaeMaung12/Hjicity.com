Welcome Everyone!

We've created this webpage to share information about Hiji's attractions, aiming to be useful for everyone.

Hiji offers a diverse range of activities suitable for all ages. From relaxing hot spring baths to scenic hiking trails, there's something here for everyone to enjoy. One of the highlights is the annual Hiji Lantern Festival, where thousands of glowing lanterns light up the town, creating a magical atmosphere.

The residents of Hiji are known for their warm hospitality and welcoming nature. You can experience the local community firsthand at the town's community centers and bustling markets, where you can meet locals and learn more about daily life in Hiji.
